Our Mission

The UAA Rocketry Team brings together passionate students from various engineering disciplines to design, build, and launch high-powered rockets. We believe in hands-on learning, collaborative problem-solving, and pushing the boundaries of student rocketry.

Propulsion

Responsible for motor selection, integration, and thrust optimization

Structures

Designs and manufactures rocket airframes and internal components

Avionics

Develops flight computers, sensors, and data collection systems

Recovery

Engineers parachute systems and safe landing mechanisms
