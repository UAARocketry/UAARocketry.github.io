Current Projects

Our team is currently working on multiple ambitious projects that push the boundaries of student rocketry.

Seawolf-1: NASA Student Launch Initiative

Our flagship project for the 2023-24 competition season. Seawolf-1 is designed to reach an altitude of 4,000 feet while carrying a complex scientific payload.

Technical Specifications

  • Length: 96 inches
  • Diameter: 6 inches
  • Motor: Aerotech L1420R
  • Target Altitude: 4,000 ft
  • Dual Deployment Recovery
  • Custom Flight Computer

Project Status

Current Phase: Manufacturing

Expected Launch: March 2024

Aurora-X: Experimental Research Platform

A research and development platform for testing advanced composite materials and novel propulsion concepts.

Technical Specifications

  • Length: 72 inches
  • Diameter: 4 inches
  • Carbon Fiber Construction
  • Modular Payload Bay
  • Advanced Telemetry System
  • Variable Motor Options

Project Status

Current Phase: Design Review

Expected Completion: Fall 2024
